Travel Guatemala is a mobile app for foreign or local tourists to discover and locate tourist places.

By combining the camera and the GPS module of your mobile device, we will offer augmented reality guidance during your travel, showing exactly in which direction to go in order to get to your desired destination.

Guatemala is rich in culture. Take 21 different indigenous Mayan populations, mix that with Spanish and European populations and influence and you get a very complex and multi-faceted national culture.

Source: http://www.guate.org/guatemala_culture.html

FAQ

For what mobile operative systems will it be available?
We are currently developing for iOS and Android systems. We plan to support Blackberry and Windows Phone after first release.
Do I need data connection in order to use the app?
Is not needed, but it's recommended for better user experience. For no data connection use, we offer a download destinations option, which the geolocations of your destination's tourist places.
